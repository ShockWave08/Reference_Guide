Can we use Farmers documents in Proof of prior Insurance?

Yes we can! If there are any lapses in the prior 6 months of policy and the document does not indicate the exact lapse, then we should use the policy number found on the document to pull up the policy from express.

Should a DMV (BOS) form have (two) 2 signatures?

Not necessarily! A DMV form can have one to none signatures. If there isn’t any area for a signature on the form and the information we need can be found, then it can be approved.

Should we ensure that all Bill of Sales have (two) 2 signatures?

Yes! Bill of Sales that aren’t DMV forms should have two (2) signatures. One from the buyer and one from the seller. Buyers can also be represented on documents as lessee and purchaser, also sellers may be represented as lesser.

One of the signatures in the Bill of Sale looks like a font style, is it acceptable?

Yes it is!

How can we identify an agent generated document?

Usually a document from an agent would come from an agency or underwritten by an agency. Some agent generated would come from lexus nexus. Some would be screen prints from express or green screen.

Which of the states are smart states?

AZ(88), AR(18), OK(08), OH(25), CT(82).

Do we have to tier the policy in green screen for smart states?

No we don’t! In the smart sates the tiers are automatically calculated.

In what states, can we use the speeding citation to tier the policy?

There is only one state where we can use the speeding indication under citation description, which is Pennsylvania (PA-06).
